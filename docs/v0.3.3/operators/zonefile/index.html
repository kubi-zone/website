Zonefile Operator

The Zonefile Operator monitors Zones for hash updates, and translates their entries field into a ConfigMap

Procedure

Whenever a Zone referenced by a ZoneFile changes, the operator rebuilds the RFC1035-compatible text representation of the zone, and creates/updates the ConfigMap with the new data.
